Normal Days

We open our shops at 9am to 10pm in the evening.

This is the time we start handling any orders and requests from our clients

You can place your orders any time on this normal days and it will be processed within the first 24hrs.

Holidays Days

On Holidays we mostly rely on our online system to pick all orders for the day. We open our shop from 10am - 10pm.

Although on some days we may close the store earlier. In these cases you can still access our services from the online system.

For online orders, you will get a customer care direct call to confirm your orders.

Offer Days

Keep your eyes open for new offers on top selling books.

Sometime vouchers might be advertised and added to the site for clients. Most vouchers appear during offer days for the store.

Ensure you subscribe to our newsletter to get updated on our daily offers.
